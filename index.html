Featured Recipe

Grilled Salmon Salad

Ingredients:

  • Salmon fillets
  • Spinach
  • Cucumber
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Avocado
  • Balsamic vinaigrette

Instructions:

  1. Grill salmon until cooked through.
  2. Combine spinach, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, and avocado in a bowl.
  3. Top with grilled salmon and drizzle with balsamic vinaigrette.
